About Us

Koppedu is a rural village located in Nindra Mandal of Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh. It is part of the Rayalaseema region and mainly depends on agriculture and allied activities for livelihood. The village follows the Gram Panchayat system for local administration and governance. Basic facilities such as primary education, local roads, and nearby market access are available through surrounding towns like Nindra and Puttur. Telugu is the primary language spoken by the residents. Koppedu reflects the typical socio-economic and cultural characteristics of a South Indian rural settlement, with a close-knit community and traditional lifestyle.
